Ruud van Nistelrooy

Rutgerus Johannes Martinus "Ruud" van Nistelrooy ( Dutch : Ruud van Nistelrooij ; [ˈryt fɑn ˈnɪstəlroːi̯]  ( listen ) ; [3] born 1 July 1976) is a retired Dutch footballer and a current manager for the PSV youth team. A former striker , he is the fourth-highest goalscorer in UEFA Champions League history with 56 goals. He is a three-time Champions League top scorer, as well as a top scorer in three different European domestic leagues.
