Radamel Falcao García Zarate (born February 10 , 1986 ) is a Colombian footballer who plays as a striker for Club Atlético River Plate of Buenos Aires , Argentina , a historic team. Falcao made his mark scoring two goals in his first start and seven in total of his first seven games. Already being classified as a rising star, Falcao tore ligaments in his knee during the offseason of 2005 which has subsequently caused him to miss an extensive amount of games. Recently, although not 100% in shape yet, Falcao scored his first goal since injury. He is currently fighting to regain his starting position.
