Zinedine Zidane

Zinedine Zidane O.L.H. , A.O.M.N. ( French pronunciation:  [zinedin zidan] ; born 23 June 1972), nicknamed " Zizou ", is a French professional football coach and former player. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time , [4] [5] [6] Zidane was an elite playmaker , renowned for his elegance, vision, ball control and technique, and played as an attacking midfielder for Cannes , Bordeaux , Juventus and Real Madrid . [7] [8] At club level, Zidane won two Serie A league titles with Juventus, before a move to Real Madrid for a world record fee of €77.5 million in 2001, which remained unmatched for the next eight years. In Spain, Zidane won the La Liga title and the UEFA Champions League , with his left-foot volleyed winner in the 2002 UEFA Champions League Final considered to be one of the greatest goals in the competition's history. [9] Zidane also won an Intercontinental Cup and a UEFA Super Cup with both teams.
