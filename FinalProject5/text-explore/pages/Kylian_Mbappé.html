Kylian Mbappé

Kylian Mbappé Lottin ( French pronunciation: ​ [kiljan (ɛ)mbape] ; born 20 December 1998) is a French professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the France national team . He is considered the world's most expensive player from a transfer value perspective by the CIES .
