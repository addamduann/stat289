Eden Hazard

Eden Michael Hazard ( French pronunciation: ​ [edɛn azaʁ] ; born 7 January 1991) is a Belgian professional footballer who plays for English club Chelsea and captains the Belgium national team . He primarily plays as an attacking midfielder and as a wide midfielder . Hazard's creativity, speed, dribbling and close ball control are widely acknowledged. [4] [5] [6] He has been described as "a superb passer" and has earned critical acclaim for his playing style, which has led to the media, coaches, and players drawing comparisons to Ballon d'Or winners Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo . [7] [8] [9] [10] Hazard is often ranked by coaches, colleagues, and commentators as one of the best players in the world. [11] [12] [13] [14] [15]
