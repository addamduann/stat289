Antoine Griezmann ( French pronunciation: ​ [ɑ̃.twan ɡʁezmɑ̃] ; born 21 March 1991) is a French footballer who currently plays for Spanish club Real Sociedad in La Liga . He plays mainly as a left winger , but can also be utilized as a striker . Griezmann is known for his ball control, quick acceleration, and dribbling and is a French youth international , having represented his country at under-19 , under-20 , and under-21 level. [1] In 2010, he was part of the team that won the 2010 UEFA European Under-19 Football Championship on home soil.
