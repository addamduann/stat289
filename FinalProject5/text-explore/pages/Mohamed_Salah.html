Mohamed Salah Ghaly (born 15 June 1992 in Basion, El Gharbia ,) is an Egyptian footballer who plays as a Winger for Swiss Super League club Basel and the Egypt national team . As well as representing the Egyptian national team at an early age, he also participated in 2011 FIFA U-20 World Cup and the 2012 Summer Olympics . He won the Swiss Super League in his first season with Basel, and was awarded the CAF Most Promising African Talent of the Year in 2012 CAF Awards . [2]
